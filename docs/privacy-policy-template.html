Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Data Collection

Todo App Calendar collects the following information:

  • Google Calendar Access Token: Stored securely in our database to enable calendar integration
  • Task Information: Tasks and todo items you create in the application
  • Email Address: Retrieved from your Google account for user identification
  • User Profile: Basic profile information from your Google account

2. Data Usage

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Create calendar events in your Google Calendar for tasks with due dates
  • Send you notifications when tasks are due (via Google Calendar reminders)
  • Improve and maintain our service
  • Provide technical support when requested

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or to provide the service you requested.

4. Google Calendar Integration

When you connect your Google Calendar:

  • We request permission to create, read, and manage calendar events
  • We only create events for tasks you explicitly set due dates for
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings or our application
  • When you disconnect, all calendar events created by our app remain in your calendar (they are not automatically deleted)

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdrawal: Disconnect Google Calendar integration at any time
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve application performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
